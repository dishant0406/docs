## Performance targets

- Proxy adds minimal latency per LLM call (tens of ms).
- SDK events are asynchronous.
- Uploads are batched.

## Privacy and security

- Optional on-prem proxy
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- PII scrubbing and retention policies
- Role-based access control

<Callout type="info">
Operational trust is as critical as technical insight.
</Callout>
